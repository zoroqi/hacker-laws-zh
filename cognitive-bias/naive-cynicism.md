# 素朴犬儒主义

认为其他人比自己更自我中心。

**Naïve Cynicism**: We believe that we observe objective reality and that other people have a higher egocentric bias than they actually do in their intentions/actions.
相信自己观察到的是客观事实，而其他人比他们表现出来的还要更加以自我为中心。
